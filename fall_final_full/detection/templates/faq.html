Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SureStep Detection, our AI-powered fall detection system.

How does SureStep Detection identify falls?

SureStep Detection leverages advanced AI models, such as YOLOv8, to analyze real-time video feeds. It accurately detects falls by recognizing patterns in movement and posture, minimizing false alarms.

What happens when a fall is detected?

Upon detecting a fall, the system automatically sends alerts to designated caregivers, family members, or emergency services. This ensures timely assistance and enhances user safety.

Can SureStep Detection work in different environments?

Yes! The system is designed to be scalable and adaptable. It can be deployed in homes, hospitals, elderly care centers, and public spaces, ensuring protection in various environments.

Does SureStep Detection require wearable devices?

No. Unlike traditional fall detection solutions that rely on wearables, SureStep Detection operates using cameras and AI-based image analysis, making it non-intrusive and hassle-free.

How secure is the system in terms of privacy?

We prioritize user privacy by ensuring that video feeds are processed securely. The system can be configured to store data locally or transmit alerts without retaining sensitive footage.
